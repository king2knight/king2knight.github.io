Introduction

 

The following collection of records, streaks, rosters, tables, and graphs was primarily derived from seasonal summaries prepared and turned in to the KSHSAA by the athletic department at McPherson High School.  This particular study starts with the 1937-38 season for the boys and with the 1969-70 season for the girls.  Although boys basketball was played prior to 1937, the high school's records only go back to the 1937-38 season.  Regardless, this was a natural place to start the study since it represented the start of Coach Jack Randle's career as head basketball coach, and it lines up closely to the records compiled by the KSHSAA.

 

In order to extract all the statistical information found in this study, originally over 1200 individual basketball games had to be entered into the computer by hand.  For each game, the following fields were entered into the relational database:  Date, Venue, Situation, Opposing Team, McPherson's Score, Opponent's Score, and the Point Difference.  In addition to the high school's seasonal summaries, information was researched via microfilm from The McPherson Daily Republican and The McPherson Sentinel to ensure accuracy and completeness.  Once all the games were entered into the computer, the database could be queried to obtain specific statistics of interest.  The type of statistics chosen for this study were patterned after university basketball media guides. Other statistics were derived because they were of specific interest to the inquiring minds of McPherson's basketball fans.

 

 

 

 

 

Although this study involved hundreds of hours of work to assemble, it pales in comparison to all the hours the coaches and players invested in practice in order to achieve the consistent level of success seen over the years.  Besides the dedication of the players and coaches, the loyal support of the McPherson Bullpup fans has had a positive effect.  Through the collective teamwork of the coaches, players, and fans, McPherson High School basketball has provided thousands of spectators with quality basketball season after season.

 

Quality basketball year after year is a luxury few schools enjoy.  McPherson is fortunate to have a sustained, rich heritage in basketball unlike any other school in the state.  As such, it is important for McPherson Bullpup fans to understand just how far back our basketball tradition goes. Having an understanding for the scope of our basketball heritage will only enhance the enjoyment and pride McPhersonites feel for their teams year after year.  Hopefully, the following collection of statistics about MHS basketball will heighten ones appreciation of the tradition known as McPherson Bullpup Basketball.

 

Now, immerse yourself in the statistics to follow.  As you study and analyze the information, you will begin to see and appreciate the magnitude of the accomplishments our players and coaches have made over the years.
