Salina Central Boys vs. MHS Boys

A Statistical Comparison

 

The Salina Central boys basketball statistics in the table below were taken from an article written by Larry Moritz of the Salina Journal on 3-7-15.  The article chronicles the successful season of the Salina Central Mustangs going into the finals of their 5A sub-state tournament against Wichita Heights.  The numerous records set this year by the Mustang boys were detailed within the article.  As a point of comparison, similar MHS records were assembled for comparison purposes and put into the table below.  The table demonstrates that success is relative.  For one program, this year's statistics are celebrated, as well they should be, and for another program it is the expected norm.
