The McPherson Roundhouse

 

Home to the McPherson Bullpups since the first game was played on December 3, 1963, the Roundhouse continues to be the premier high school facility in the state of Kansas.  It has hosted numerous CKL (Central Kansas League) and AVL (Ark Valley League) league games as well as non-league games, the annual McPherson Invitational Tournament and many Sub-State games.

 

In addition to its unique design and great atmosphere for basketball, the Roundhouse has been kind to the home team over the last 55 seasons.  The overall winning percentage at the Roundhouse has been 86.1% with an average victory margin of 19.1 points per game!
