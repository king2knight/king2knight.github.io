Note:

 

Between the 38 game Roundhouse winning streak and the subsequent 66 game Roundhouse winning streak, McPherson lost a close home game 50-52 to Newton High School on January 14, 1992. The game was close throughout with Newton leading by 5 points after the first quarter, trailing by 2 at the half, and up again by 2 points at the end of the third quarter.  With 5 seconds to go in the game and trailing Newton by one point, McPherson was at the free throw line for 2 shots. One make to tie, and two makes to win.  Things looked good as our best free throw shooter was at the line.  But, he missed both free throws and the 38 game winning streak was over.  For the game, MHS only made 6 out 16 free throw attempts.  After that loss, McPherson reeled off a 66 game winning streak in the Roundhouse.  Had McPherson pulled out a win that night with Newton, the winning streak at the Roundhouse would have stood at 105 games.
