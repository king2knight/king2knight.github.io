The Opposing Men's Teams

 

Since 1937, McPherson High School has played 112 different schools and has been successful against the majority of those schools. Out of the 112 teams played, only the 16 schools listed in the table below have a winning record against McPherson. Of those 16 schools, 7 would have been considered 6A schools by today’s classification system and only two schools have played McPherson more than 9 times. Six of the teams we have not played in over 40 years.
