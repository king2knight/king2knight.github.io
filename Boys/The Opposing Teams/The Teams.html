The Teams

 

Information compiled about team members and individual lettermen was taken from the official record of school athletics at McPherson High School.  These records were based upon the decisions and judgments made by the respective head coaches from 1937 to the present.

 

For example, to be listed as a team member in the Seasonal Summaries, an individual must have been either a letterman or provisional letterman in that season.  Being a letterman was determined by the coach based upon actual varsity playing time accrued.  While other individuals were on the team as non-lettermen, their contributions to that team's seasonal record were minimal due to their limited playing time.

 

The table of All-Time Lettermen was based upon the official record of athletics.  Any player awarded either a letter or provisional letter was listed in the table.  Each player was listed in alphabetical order followed by their year of graduation.  The actual year in which the letter was awarded was not denoted because some players lettered prior to their senior year, but did not play as a senior.
