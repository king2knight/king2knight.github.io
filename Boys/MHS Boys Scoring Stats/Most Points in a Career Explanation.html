Most Points in a Career Explanation

 

 

I made some suggested changes (in red ink) to the years played for the career scorers metric. My rationale for the changes was based on the following:

 

I used the yearly summaries from the school's official records which identify who was issued either a provisional or varsity letter. My assumption is that if a player received a letter of any type in a given season, then he may have scored in that season.

 

For example, Christian Ulsaker lettered in the following seasons: 2007-08, 2008-09, 2009-10, and 2010-11. Although I don't track individuals, it would seem possible Christian played in some games in December of 2007 given he played in 85 games in his career. Using this rationale, I suggested his playing years should be 2007-11 rather than 2008-11.
