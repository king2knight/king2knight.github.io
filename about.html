About This Exhibit

This exhibit was donated as a collaboration project on behalf of Mark Heidebrecht and Johnathan King. The data and records contained in this exhibit are the result of dedicated research and compiling by Mark. Information was obtained through official school records, yearbooks, newspapers, and archived microfilm. The graphs and charts were also created by Mark using Harvard Graphics. The kiosk was written and configured by Johnathan using the Start Bootstrap framework and used under the MIT license.
